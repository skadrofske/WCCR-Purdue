Rock the Quad

Rock the Quad has been a completely free and student-run concert on Slayter Hill every spring semester, in mid-April, since the early 2000â€™s. Past artists have included Smallpools, New Politics, Red Jumpsuit Apparatus, Gym Class Heroes, and many more. Recently, Rock the Quad 2017 performances included Vesperteen, Shiny Penny, and The Griswolds. RTQ 2018 performers were Future Thieves & Airways. RTQ 2019 were Jukebox the Ghost and Houndmouth.

The planning for Rock the Quad 2020 is in full swing! Our Concert Coordinating committee has a big hand in the production of the event. However, our concert would not be possible without the help of organizations around Purdue. If your organization is interested in becoming a sponsor for Rock the Quad 2020, email wccrpurdue@gmail.com with the subject 'Sponsorship Opportunities' and we will give you more information about our sponsorship packages. Thank you to all of our continued partners!
